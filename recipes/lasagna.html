Lasagna

Description

Always though making a delicious plate of lasagna was a gigantic effort? Think again!

Follow this step by step recipe and surprise your guest with smoking hot dish of lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound of Italian sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 36 ounces of pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. First, preheat your oven to 350°F. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of salted water until al dente according to package directions. Drain, rinse under cold water, and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or dutch oven, brown the beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium-high heat until no pink remains. Drain any fat. Stir in the pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, ½ teaspoon of salt, and ¼ teaspoon of black pepper. Simmer uncovered over medium heat for 5 minutes or until thickened.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine 1 ½ cups mozzarella, ¼ cup parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, egg, and ¼ teaspoon salt.
  4. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in a 9×13 pan or casserole dish. Top it with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with ⅓ of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and sprinkle with the remaining 2 ½ cups mozzarella cheese and ¼ cup parmesan cheese. Bake for an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly.
  6. Let the lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes before cutting. Enjoy!
